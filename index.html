intimacy_device

Exploring how intimacy can be mediated through technology

paper code

Intimacy is an essential part of human relationships.
Being physically apart makes it harder to nourish our relationships.
While modern technology tries to solve this issue, the available communication methods still lacks real intimacy.

With this project I am to explore how existing research projects within this design space relate to intimacy, in order to design and prototype a better solution.
